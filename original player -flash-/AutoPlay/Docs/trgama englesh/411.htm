Nobel Quran - English Translation
1. Alif, Lam, Mim.
2. These are the verses of the Book, full of wisdom,
3. A guidance and a mercy for those who do good,
4. Those who observe Prayer and pay the Zakat and who have firm faith in the Hereafter.
5. It is they who follow guidance from their Lord, and it is they who shall prosper.
6. And of men is he who takes idle tales in exchange for guidance to lead men astray from the path of ALLAH, without knowledge, and to make fun of it. For such there will be humiliating punishment.
7. And when Our Signs are recited to him, he turns away disdainfully, as though he heard them not, as if there were a heaviness in his ears. So announce to him a painful punishment.
8. Surely, those who believe and do good works - they will have Gardens of Delight,
9. Wherein they will abide. ALLAH has made a true promise; and HE is the Mighty, the Wise.
10. HE has created the heavens without any pillars that you can see, and HE has placed in the earth firm mountains that it may not quake with you, and HE has spread therein all kinds of creatures; and WE have sent down water from the clouds, and have caused to grow therein of every fine species.
11. This is the creation of ALLAH. Now show me what others beside HIM have created. Nay, but the wrongdoers are in manifest error.
