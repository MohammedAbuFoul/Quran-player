Nobel Quran - English Translation
41. And a Sign for them is that WE carry their offspring in the laden ships.
42. And WE will create for them the like thereof whereon they will ride.
43. And if WE so willed, WE could drown them; then they would have no one to succour them, nor would they be rescued,
44. Except through mercy from US and a provision for a time.
45. And when it is said to them, `Guard yourselves against that which is before you through Prayer and that which is behind you through repentance, that you may receive mercy,' they turn away.
46. And there comes not to them any Sign out of the Signs of their Lord, but they turn away from it.
47. And when it is said to them, `Spend out of that which ALLAH has given you,' those who disbelieve say to those who believe, `Shall we feed him whom ALLAH would have fed, if HE had so willed ? You are but in manifest error.'
48. And they say, `When will this promise of punishment be fulfilled, if you are truthful ?'
49. They are waiting only for a single blast which will seize them while they are still disputing.
50. And they will not be able to make a will nor will they return to their families.
51. And the trumpet shall be blown, and lo ! from the graves they will hasten on to their Lord.
52. They will say to one another, `O woe to us ! who has raised us from our place of sleep ? This is what the Gracious God had promised, and the Messengers, indeed, spoke the truth.'
53. It will be one blast and lo ! they will all be brought before US.
54. And on that day no soul will be wronged in aught; nor will you be requited but for what you used to do.
