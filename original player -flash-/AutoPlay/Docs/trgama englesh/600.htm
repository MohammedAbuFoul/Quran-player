Nobel Quran - English Translation
10. And that which is hidden in the breasts is brought forth ?
11. Surely, their Lord will, on that day, be fully Aware of them.
 
1. The Great Calamity !
2. What is the great calamity ?
3. And what should make thee know what the Great Calamity is ?
4. The day when men will be like scattered moths,
5. And the mountains will be like carded wool.
6. Then, as for him whose scales are heavy,
7. He will have a pleasant life.
8. But as for him whose scales are light,
9. Hell will be a nursing mother to him.
10. And what should make thee know what that is ?
11. It is a blazing Fire.
 
1. Mutual rivalry in seeking increase in worldly possessions diverts you from God,
2. Till you reach the graves.
3. Nay ! you will soon come to know the Truth.
4. Nay again ! you will soon come to know.
5. Nay ! if you only knew with certain knowledge;
6. You will surely see Hell in this very life.
7. Aye, you will surely see it with the eye of certainty Hereafter.
8. Then, on that day you shall be called to account for the favours bestowed upon you.
