Nobel Quran - English Translation
84. `And grant me true and lasting reputation among posterity;
85. `And make me one of the heirs of the Garden of Bliss;
86. `And forgive my father, for he is of the erring ones;
87. `And disgrace me not on the day when they will be raised up,
88. `The day when wealth and sons shall not avail;
89. `Save him who comes to ALLAH with a sound heart.'
90. And Heaven shall be brought near to the righteous.
91. And Hell shall be placed in full view to those who have gone astray.
92. And it will be said to them, `Where are those that you worshiped
93. `Beside ALLAH ? Can they help you or help themselves ?'
94. Then will they be thrown headlong therein, they and those who have gone astray,
95. And the Hosts of Iblis, all together.
96. They will say, whilst they dispute between themselves therein,
97. `By ALLAH, we were in manifest error,
98. `When we held you as equal with the Lord of the worlds,
99. `And none led us astray but the guilty ones,
100. `And now we have no intercessors,
101. `Nor any warm friend;
102. `Could we but return to the world, we would be among the believers !'
103. In this, verily, there is a Sign; but most of these would not believe.
104. And verily thy Lord - HE is the Mighty, the Merciful.
105. The people of Noah treated the Messengers as liars,
106. When their brother Noah said to them, `Will you not be God-Fearing ?
107. `Surely I am unto you a Messenger, faithful to my trust
108. `So fear ALLAH and obey me;
109. `And I ask you no reward for it. My reward is only with the Lord of the worlds;
110. `So fear ALLAH and obey me;
111. They said, `Shall we believe, in thee when it is the meanest of us who follow thee ?'
