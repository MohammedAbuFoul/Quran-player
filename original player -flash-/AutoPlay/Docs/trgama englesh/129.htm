Nobel Quran - English Translation
9. And if WE had appointed as Messenger an angel, WE would have made him appear as a man; and thus WE would have caused to be confused to them that which they are themselves confusing.
10. And surely the Messengers have been mocked at before thee, but that which they mocked at encompassed those of them who scoffed at it.
11. Say, `Go about in the earth and see what was the end of those who treated the Prophets as liars.'
12. Say, `To whom belongs what is in the heavens and the earth ?' Say, `To ALLAH.' HE has taken oupon Himself to show mercy. HE will certainly continue to assemble you till the Day of Resurrection. There is no doubt in it. Those who have ruined their souls will not believe.
13. To HIM belongs whatever exists in the darkness of the night and the light of the day. And HE is All-Hearing, the All-Knowing.
14. Say, `Shall I take any protector other than ALLAH, the Maker of the heavens and the earth, Who feeds and is not fed ?' Say, `I have been commanded to be the first of those who submit.' And be thou not of those who associate partners with ALLAH.
15. Say, `Indeed I fear, if I disobey my Lord, the punishment of an awful day.'
16. He from whom it is averted on that day, ALLAH indeed has had mercy on him. And that indeed is a manifest achievement.
17. And if ALLAH touch thee with affliction, there is none to remove it but HE; and if HE touch thee with happiness, then HE has the power to do all that HE wills.
18. And HE is supreme over HIS servants; and HE is the Wise, the All-Aware.
