Nobel Quran - English Translation
1. Say `He is ALLAH, the One !
2. ALLAH the Independent and Besought of all.
3. `HE begets not, nor, is HE begotten,
4. And there is none like unto HIM.
 
1. Say, I seek refuge in the Lord of the dawn,
2. `From the evil of that which HE has created,
3. `And from the evil of darkness when it overspreads,
4. And from the evil of those who blow upon the knots of mutual relationships to undo them,
5. `And from the evil of the envier when he envies.'
 
1. Say, `I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind,
2. `The King of mankind,
3. `The God of mankind,
4. `From the evil whisperings of the sneaking whisperer;
5. `Who whispers into the hearts of men,
6. `From among jinn and men.'
