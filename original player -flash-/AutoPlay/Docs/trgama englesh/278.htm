Nobel Quran - English Translation
94. And make not your oaths a means of deceit between you; or you foot will slip after it has been firmly established and you will taste evil because by acting thus you have turned people away from the path of ALLAH, and you will have a severe punishment.
95. And barter not the covenant of ALLAH for a paltry price. Surely, that which is with ALLAH is better for you if you knew.
96. That which you have shall pass away, but that which is with ALLAH is lasting. And WE will certainly give those, who are steadfast, their reward according to the best of their works.
97. Whoso acts righteously, whether male or female and is a believer, WE will surely grant him a pure life; and WE will surely bestow on such their reward according to the best of their works.
98. And when thou recitest the Qur'an, seek refuge with ALLAH from Satan, the rejected.
99. Surely he has no power over those who believe and who put their trust in their Lord.
100. His power is only over those who make friends with him and those who associate partners with him.
101. And when WE bring one Sign in place of another - and ALLAH knows best the object of what HE reveals - they say, `Thou art but a fabricator,' Nay, but most of them know not.
102. Say, `The Spirit of holiness has brought it down from thy Lord with truth, that HE may strengthen in their faith those who believe and as a guidance and glad tidings for Muslims.
