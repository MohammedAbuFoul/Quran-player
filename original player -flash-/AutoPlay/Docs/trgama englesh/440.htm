Nobel Quran - English Translation
45. And if ALLAH were to punish people for what they do, HE would not leave a living creature on the surface of the earth; but HE gives them respite until an appointed term; and when their appointed time comes they find that ALLAH has all HIS servants well under HIS eyes.
 
1. Ya Sin.
2. By the Qur'an, full of wisdom,
3. Thou art, indeed, one of the Messengers,
4. On the right path.
5. This is a revelation from the Mighty, the Merciful.
6. That thou mayest warn a people whose fathers were not warned, and so they are heedless.
7. Surely, the word has proved true against most of them, for they believe not.
8. WE have put round their necks collars reaching unto their chins, so that their heads are raised up.
9. And WE have set a barrier before them and a barrier behind them, and have covered them over, so that they cannot see.
10. Alike it is to them whether thou warn them or warn them not; they will not believe.
11. Thou canst warn only him who would follow the Reminder and fear the Gracious God in secret. So give him the glad tidings of forgiveness and a noble reward.
12. Surely, WE alone give life to the dead, and WE write down that which they send forward and that which they leave behind; and all things WE have recorded in a clear Book.
