Nobel Quran - English Translation
112. He said, `And what knowledge have I as to what they have been doing ?
113. `Their account is only with my Lord, if you only knew;
114. `And I am not going to drive away the believers;
115. `I am only a plain Warner.'
116. They said, `If thou desist not, O Noah, thou shalt surely be stoned to death.'
117. He said, `My Lord, my people have treated me as a liar;
118. `Therefore judge Thou decisively between me and them; and save me and the believers that are with me.'
119. So WE saved him and those who were with him in the fully laden Ark.
120. Then WE drowned thereafter those who remained behind.
121. In this, verily, there is a Sign; but most of them would not believe.
122. And verily thy Lord - HE is the Mighty, the Merciful.
123. The tribe of Ad rejected the Messengers,
124. When their Brother Hud said to them, `Will you not fear God ?
125. `Surely, I am unto you a Messenger, faithful to my trust;
126. `So fear ALLAH and obey me;
127. `And I ask of you no reward for it. My reward is only with the Lord of the worlds;
128. `Do you build monuments on every high place, seeking vain glory ?
129. `And do you erect palaces as if you will live for ever ?
130. `And when you lay hands upon anyone, you lay hands as tyrants.
131. `So fear ALLAH and obey me;
132. `And fear HIM Who has helped you with all that you know;
133. `He has helped you with cattle and sons,
134. `And gardens and springs;
135. `Indeed I fear for you the punishment of an awful day.'
136. They said, `It is the same to us whether thou admonish us or whether thou be not of those who admonish;
