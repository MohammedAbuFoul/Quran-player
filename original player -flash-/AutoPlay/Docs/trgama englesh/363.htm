Nobel Quran - English Translation
33. And they do not come to thee with an objection but WE provide thee with the truth and an excellent explanation.
34. Those who will be gathered unto Hell on their faces -they will be the worst in plight and most astray from the right path.
35. WE gave Moses the Book, and appointed with him his brother Aaron as his assistant.
36. And WE said to them, `Go both of you to the people who have rejected OUR Signs;' Then WE destroyed them utterly.
37. And the people of Noah, when they rejected the Messengers, WE drowned them, and WE made them a Sign for mankind. And WE have prepared a painful punishment for the wrongdoers.
38. And WE destroyed Ad and Thamud, and the People of the Well, and many a generation between them,
39. And to each one WE set forth examples; and each one WE utterly destroyed.
40. And these Meccans must have visited the town whereon was rained an evil rain. Have they not seen it ? Nay, they expect not to be raised after death.
41. And when they see thee, they only make a jest of thee, and say, `What ! is this he whom ALLAH has sent as a Messenger ?
42. `He indeed had well-nigh led us astray from our gods, had we not steadily adhered to them.' And they shall no, when they see the punishment, who is most astray from the right path.
43. Hast thou seen him who takes his own evil desire for his god ? Canst thou be a guardian over them ?
