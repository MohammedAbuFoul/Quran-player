Nobel Quran - English Translation
1. Say, `O ye disbelievers !
2. `I worship not as you worship,
3. `Nor do you worship as I worship.
4. `Nor do I worship those that you worship,
5. `Nor do you worship Him Whom I worship.
6. `For you your religion, and for me my religion.
 
1. When the help of ALLAH comes and the Victory,
2. And thou seest men entering the religion of ALLAH in troops,
3. Glorify thy Lord with HIS praise and seek HIS forgiveness. Surely HE is Oft-returning with mercy.
 
1. Perish the two hands of Abu Lahab, and perish he !
2. His wealth and what he has earned shall avail him naught,
3. Soon shall he burn in a flaming fire;
4. And his wife, too, bearer of slander,
5. Round her neck shall be a halter of twisted palm-fibre.
