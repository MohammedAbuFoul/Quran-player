Nobel Quran - English Translation
49. And remember the time when WE delivered you from Pharaoh's people who afflicted you with grievous torment, slaying your sons and sparing your women; and in that was a great trial for you from your Lord.
50. And remember also the time when WE divided the sea for you and saved you and drowned Pharaoh's people while you looked on.
51. And when WE made Moses a promise of forty nights, then you took the calf for worship in his absence and you were the transgressors.
52. Then WE forgave you thereafter, that you may be grateful.
53. And remember when WE gave Moses the Book and the Discrimination, that you may be rightly guided.
54. And when Moses said to his people, `O my people, you have indeed wronged yourselves by taking the calf for worship; turn ye therefore to your Maker, and kill your evil desires; that is best for you in the sight of your Maker.' Then HE turned towards you with compassion. Surely HE is Oft-Returning with compassion and is Merciful.
55. And remember when you said, `O Moses, we will not believe thee until we see ALLAH face to face;' Then the thunderbolt overtook you, while you witnessed with your own eyes the consequences of your conduct.
56. The We raised you up after your death that you might be grateful.
57. And WE caused the clouds to be a shade over you and sent down on you Manna and Salwa, saying, `Eat of the good things We have provided for you.' And they wronged us not, but it was themselves that they wronged.
