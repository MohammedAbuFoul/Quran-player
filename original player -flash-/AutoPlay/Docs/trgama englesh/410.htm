Nobel Quran - English Translation
51. And if WE had sent a wind and they saw their harvest turn yellow, they would, certainly thereafter, begin to deny Our favours.
52. And thou canst not make the dead to hear, nor canst thou make the deaf to hear the call, when they retreat turning their backs;
53. Nor canst thou guide the blind out of their error. Thou canst make only those to hear who would believe in Our Signs and submit.
54. ALLAH is HE Who created you in a state of weakness, and after weakness gave you strength; then, after strength, caused weakness and old age. HE creates what HE pleases. HE is the All-Knowing, the All-Powerful.
55. And on the day when the appointed Hour shall arrive, the guilty will swear that they tarried not save a brief period - thus are they turned away from the right path.
56. But those, who are given knowledge and faith, will say, `You have indeed tarried according to the Book of ALLAH, till the Day of Resurrection. And this indeed is the Day of Resurrection, but you did not care to know.
57. So on that day their excuses will not avail the wrongdoers; nor will they be allowed to make amends.
58. And, truly, WE have set forth for men in this Qur'an every type of parable, and indeed, if thou bring them a Sign, those who disbelieve will, surely, say, `You are but liars.'
59. Thus does ALLAH seal the hearts of those who have no knowledge.
60. So be thou patient. Surely, the promise of ALLAH is true; and let not those who have no certainty of faith hold thee in light estimation so as to move thee from the stand thou hast taken.
