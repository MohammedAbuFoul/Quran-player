Nobel Quran - English Translation
6. ALLAH has made this promise. ALLAH breaks not HIS promise, but most men know not.
7. They know only the outer part of the life of this world, and of the Hereafter they are utterly unmindful.
8. Do they not reflect within themselves that ALLAH has not created the heavens and the earth and all that is between the two but in accordance with the requirements of wisdom and for a fixed term ? But many among men believe not in the meeting with their Lord.
9. Have they not traveled in the earth so that they might see how evil was the end of those who were before them ? They were stronger than these in power, and they tilled the soil and populated it more and better than these have populated it. And their Messengers came to them with manifest Signs. And ALLAH would not wrong them, but they wronged their own souls.
10. Then evil was the end of those who did evil, because, they rejected the Signs of ALLAH and scoffed at them.
11. ALLAH originates creation; then HE keeps repeating it; then to HIM shall you be brought back.
12. And on the day when the Hour will arrive the guilty shall be seized with despair.
13. And they shall have no intercessors from among those who they associate with ALLAH; and they will deny those whom they associate with HIM.
14. And on the day when the Hour will arrive - on that day they will become separated from one another.
15. Then those who believed and acted righteously will be honoured and made happy in stately gardens.
