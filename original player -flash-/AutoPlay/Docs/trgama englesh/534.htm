Nobel Quran - English Translation
68. In both of them there will be all kinds of fruit, and dates and pomegranates.
69. Which, then, of the favours of your Lord will ye twain, deny ?
70. Therein will be maidens, good and beautiful. -
71. Which, then, of the favours of your Lord will ye twain, deny ?
72. Fair maidens with lovely black eyes, guarded in pavilions
73. Which, then, of the favours of your Lord will ye twain, deny ?
74. Whom neither man nor jinn will have touched before them -
75. Which, then, of the favours of your Lord will ye twain, deny ?
76. Reclining on green cushions and beautiful carpets.
77. Which, then, of the favours of your Lord will ye twain, deny ?
78. Blessed is the name of thy Lord, Master of Glory and Honour.
 
1. When the Inevitable event comes to pass -
2. There is no denying its coming to pass -
3. Some it will bring low, others it will exalt.
4. When the earth will be shaken with a terrible shaking;
5. And mountains will be scattered - a complete scattering.
6. They shall all become like dust particles scattered about.
7. And you shall be divided into three groups:
8. Those on the right hand - how lucky are those on the right hand !
9. And those on the left hand - how unlucky are those on the left hand !
10. And the foremost; they are truly the foremost;
11. They will be those near to God;
12. And will be in the Gardens of Bliss -
13. A large party from among the first believers,
14. And a few from among the later ones,
15. Seated on couches inwrought with gold and jewels,
16. Reclining thereon, facing each other.
