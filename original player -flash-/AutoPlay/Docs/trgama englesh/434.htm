Nobel Quran - English Translation
49. Say, `The truth has come and falsehood could neither originate anything, nor reproduce it'
50. Say, If I err, I err only against myself; and if I am rightly guided, it is because of what my Lord revealed to me. Verily, HE is All-Hearing, Nigh.'
51. Couldst thou but see when they will be smitten with fear ? Then there will be no escape, and they will be seized from a place nearby.
52. And they will say, `We now believe therein.' But how can they attain faith from a position so far off ?
53. They had disbelieved in it before, while they indulged in conjectures with regard to the unseen from a far off-place.
54. And a barrier will be placed between them and that which they long for, as was done with the like of them before. They too were in disquieting doubt.
 
1. All praise belongs to ALLAH, the originator of the heavens and the earth, Who employs the angels as Messengers, having wings, two, three, and four. HE adds to HIS creation whatever HE pleases; for ALLAH has power over all things.
2. Whatever sources of mercy ALLAH lays open for mankind -there is none to withhold them; and whatever of such sources HE withholds, there is none who can release them after that; and HE is the Mighty, the Wise.
3. O ye men, remember the favour of ALLAH that HE has bestowed upon you. Is there any creator other than ALLAH who provides for you from the heaven and the earth ? There is none worthy of worship but HE. Wither then are you turned away ?
