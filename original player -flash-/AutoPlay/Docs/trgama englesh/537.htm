Nobel Quran - English Translation
77. This is, indeed, a noble Qur'an,
78. In a well-preserved Book.
79. Which none shall touch except those who are purified.
80. It is a revelation from the Lord of the worlds.
81. Is it this divine discourse that you would reject,
82. And do you make the denial thereof your means of livelihood ?
83. Why, then, when the soul of the dying man reaches the throat,
84. And you are at that moment looking on -
85. And WE are nearer to him than you, but you see not -
86. Why, then, if you are not to be called to account,
87. You cannot bring it back if you are truthful ?
88. Now if he be of those who are near to God,
89. Then for him is comfort and fragrance of happiness and Garden of bliss;
90. And if he be of those of the right hand,
91. Then, `Peace be ever on thee, O thou, of those of the right hand !'
92. But if he be of those who reject the truth and are in error,
93. Then for him will be an entertainment of boiling water,
94. And burning in Hell.
95. Verily, this is the certain truth.
96. So glorify the name of thy Lord, the Incomparably Great.
 
1. Whatever is in the heavens and the earth glorifies ALLAH; and HE is the Mighty, the Wise.
2. HIS is the Kingdom of the heavens and the earth; HE gives life and HE causes death, and HE has power over all things.
3. HE is the First and the Last, and the Manifest and the Hidden, and HE has full knowledge of all things.
