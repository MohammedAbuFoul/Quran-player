Nobel Quran - English Translation
50. The Keepers will say, `Did not your Messengers come to you with manifest Signs ?' They will say, `Yea.' The Keepers will say, `Then pray on.' but the prayer of disbelievers is of no avail.
51. Most, surely, we help Our Messengers and those who believe, both in the present life and on the day when the witnesses will stand forth,
52. The day when their pleading will not profit the wrongdoers, and for them will be the curse and for them will be the evil abode.
53. And, indeed, WE gave Moses the guidance, and made the children of Israel heirs to the Book -
54. A guidance and a Reminder for men of understanding.
55. So have patience. Surely, the promise of ALLAH is true. And ask forgiveness for them for the wrongs they have done thee and glorify thy Lord with HIS praise in the evening and the in morning.
56. Those who dispute concerning the Signs of ALLAH without any authority having come to them from ALLAH - there is nothing in their breasts but an ambition to become great which they will never attain. So seek refuge in ALLAH. Surely, HE is the All-Hearing, the All-Seeing.
57. Certainly, the creation of the heavens and the earth is greater than the creation of mankind; but most men know not.
58. And the blind and the seeing are not equal; neither are those who believe and do good deeds equal to those who do evil. Little do you reflect.
