Nobel Quran - English Translation
207. Of what avail will be to them that which they were allowed to enjoy ?
208. And never did WE destroy any township but it had its Warners.
209. So that they may be admonished; and WE are not unjust.
210. And the evil ones have not brought it down;
211. It does neither suit them nor have they the power to produce it.
212. Surely, they are debarred from listening to the Divine Word.
213. Call not, therefore, on any other god beside ALLAH, lest thou become one of those who are punished,
214. And warn thy nearest kinsmen,
215. And lower thy wing of mercy to the believers who follow thee.
216. Then if they disobey thee, say, `I repudiate all connection with what you do.'
217. And put thy trust in the Mighty, the Merciful,
218. Who sees thee when thou standest in Prayer,
219. And Who sees thy movements among those who prostrate themselves before ALLAH.
220. HE is indeed the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing.
221. Shall I inform you on whom the evil ones descend ?
222. They descend on every lying sinner.
223. They strain their ears towards heaven, and most of them are liars.
224. And as for the poets - it is the erring ones who follow them.
225. Dost thou not see how they wonder aimlessly in every valley,
226. And that they say what they do not ?
227. Save those who believe and do righteous deeds, and remember ALLAH much, and defend themselves after they are wronged. And the wrongdoers shall soon know to what place of return they shall return.
