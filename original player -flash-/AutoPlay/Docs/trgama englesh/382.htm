Nobel Quran - English Translation
56. But the only answer of his people was that they said, `Drive out Lot's family from your city. They are a people who would keep pure.
57. So WE saved him and his family, except his wife; her WE decreed to be of those who stayed behind.
58. And WE rained upon them a rain; and evil was the rain that descended upon those who had been warned.
59. Say, `All praise belongs to ALLAH, and peace be upon those servants of HIS whom HE has chosen. Is ALLAH better or that which they associate with HIM ?
60. Or, Who created the heavens and the earth, and Who sent down water for you from the sky wherewith WE cause to grow beautiful orchards ? You could not cause their trees to grow. Is there a god with ALLAH ? Nay, they are a people who deviate from the right path.
61. Or, Who made the earth a place of rest, and placed rivers in its midst, and placed upon it firm mountains, and put a barrier between the two waters ? Is there a god with ALLAH ? Nay, most of them know not.
62. Or, Who answers the cry of the distressed person when he calls upon HIM, and removes the evil, and makes you successors in the earth ? Is there a god with ALLAH ? Little is that you reflect.
63. Or, Who guides you in the depths of darkness on land and sea, and Who sends the winds as glad tidings before HIS mercy ? Is there a god with ALLAH ? Exalted is ALLAH above what they associate with HIM.
