Nobel Quran - English Translation
1. By the heaven and the Morning Star -
2. And what should make thee know what the Morning Star is ?
3. It is the star of piercing brightness -
4. There is not a soul but has a guardian over it.
5. So let man consider of what he is created.
6. He is created of a gushing fluid,
7. Which issues forth from between the loins and the breastbones.
8. Surely, ALLAH has the power to bring him back to life,
9. On the day when secrets shall be disclosed.
10. And he shall have no strength and no helper.
11. By the cloud which gives rain repeatedly,
12. And by the earth which opens out with herbage.
13. Surely, the Qur'an is a decisive word.
14. And it is not a vain talk.
15. Surely, they plan a plan,
16. And I also plan a plan.
17. So give respite to the disbelievers. Aye respite them for a little while.
 
1. Glorify the name of thy Lord, the Most High,
2. Who creates man and perfects him.
3. And Who determines his capacities and furnishes him with appropriate guidance.
4. And Who brings forth the pasturage,
5. Then turns it into black stubble.
6. WE shall teach thee the Qur'an and thou shalt not forget it,
7. Except as what ALLAH wills. Surely, HE knows what is manifest and what is hidden.
8. And WE shall provide thee with every facility.
9. So keep on admonishing people. Surely, admonition is, indeed, profitable.
10. He who fears will heed;
11. But the most wretched will turn aside from it,
12. He who will enter the great Fire.
13. Then he will neither die therein nor live.
14. Verily, he, indeed, will prosper who purifies himself,
15. And remembers the name of his Lord and offer Prayers.
