Nobel Quran - English Translation
13. And I Myself have chosen thee; so hearken to what is revealed to thee;
14. `Verily , I am ALLAH; there is no god but I, so worship ME alone and observe Prayer for my remembrance;
15. Surely, the Hour is coming and I am going to manifest it, that every soul may be recompensed for its labours;
16. `So let not him, who believes not therein and follows his own low desires, turn thee away therefrom, lest thou perish;
17. `And what is that in thy right hand, O Moses ?'
18. Moses said, `This is my rod, I lean on it and beat down therewith leaves for my sheep and other uses also I find in it.'
19. God said, `Cast it down, O Moses.'
20. So he cast it down, and behold ! it was a serpent running.
21. God said, `Take hold of it, and fear not. WE shall restore it to its former state.
22. `And draw thy hand closer under thy arm-pit, it shall come forth white, without any disease - another Sign.
23. `That WE may show thee some of OUR greater Signs;
24. `Go thou to Pharaoh; he has indeed exceeded all bounds.'
25. Moses said, `My Lord, expand for me my breast;
26. `And make my task easy for me;
27. `And loose the knot from my tongue,
28. `That they may understand my speech;
29. `And grant me an assistant from my family -
30. `Aaron, my brother;
31. `Increase my strength by him;
32. `And make him share my task;
33. `That we may glorify thee much;
34. `And remember thee much;
35. Thou art, surely, Ever-Watching over us.'
36. God said, `Granted is thy prayer, O Moses.
37. `And WE did indeed confer a favour upon thee at another time also;
