Nobel Quran - English Translation
1. Surely, WE sent it down during the Night of Decree.
2. And what shall make thee know what the Night of Decree is ?
3. The Night of Decree is better than a thousand months.
4. Therein descend angels and the Spirit by the command of their Lord with Divine decree concerning every matter.
5. It is all peace till the rising of the dawn.
 
1. Those, who disbelieve from among the People of the Book and the idolaters, would not desist from disbelief until there should come to them the clear evidence -
2. A Messenger from ALLAH, reciting unto them the pure Scriptures,
3. Wherein are lasting commandments.
4. And those to whom the Book was given did not become divided until after clear evidence had come to them.
5. And they were not commanded but to serve ALLAH, being sincere to HIM in obedience, and being upright, and to observe Prayer and pay the Zakat. And that is the right religion.
6. Verily, those who disbelieve from among the People of the Book and the idolaters, will be in the Fire of Hell, abiding therein. They are the worst of creatures.
7. Verily, those who believe and do righteous deeds they are the best of creatures.
